The howling wind and relentless rain batter the exterior of the isolated mansion as Detective Jonathan Hart and his assistant Evelyn Hart step out of their car. Jonathan, a seasoned investigator with a sharp mind and unwavering determination, adjusts his coat against the storm, his face set in a focused expression. They approach the grand, creaking door, summoned to this remote estate to unravel a bizarre murder that has already sent shockwaves through the small community. As the door swings open, revealing a group of anxious faces inside, the storm slams it shut behind them, trapping everyone within the walls of this enigma-laden house.
