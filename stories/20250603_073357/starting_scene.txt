The rain lashes against the windows of the old stone mansion as Detective Jonathan Hart steps out of the car, his coat collar turned up against the chill. Evelyn Hart, his ever-reliable assistant, follows closely, carrying a briefcase of investigative tools. They have been summoned to this remote estate in the English countryside to investigate a bizarre murder reported by the household. As the door creaks open, revealing a group of anxious faces inside, the storm howls louder, and the bridge washing out traps everyone within these walls. Jonathan Hart, seasoned detective, knows that with a killer likely among them, the night is far from over.
